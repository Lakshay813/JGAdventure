Solang – Beas Kund Trek

Location:             Manali, Distt. Kullu (Himachal)

Base:                   Solang Valley

Grade:                 Moderate

Altitude:              3540 m

Best Time:           April to November

Suitable For:       15 Yrs and above

 

 

Trip Introduction

The ancient Indians called the Beas River as Vipas or Arjiki while the ancient Greeks called it as Hyphasis. The current name Beas is regarded as formed from Vyas who is the author of Mahabharata, the great Indian epic, however some scholars in Himachal Pradesh call this river as Vipasha.

 

ABOUT PLACES COVERED:

 

Manali: Geographic coordinates : 10.57° N 72.62° E

As the earth slowly dried of water, here arose a place of breath taking natural beauty - which was only appropriate for it was at Manali that life began again. And today this legendary cradle of all human kind is a prime holiday destination. This Himalayan mountain range is loved by those who are nature seekers and are looking for multiplicity of sights and treks like difficult snow clad region of Rohtang at one theme to Hot water springs at Vashishtha Village and Manikaran Saheb at the other. Manali is recognized with fast flowing Beas river passing through the entire length and with fascinating beauty of mountain scapes, matchless snow views all around. This valley full of rich fauna and flora.

 

Solang Valley: One of the most beautiful meadows in the region, Solan Nullah is the starting point to many trek during summer and Himachal Pradesh's best ski slope. Set up camp for overnight stay. Rest of the day at leisure to explore and enjoy the tranquility of the mountain.

 

Dhundi: Dhundi is another Alpine meadow full of wild flowers.

 

Beas Kund: Beas so named after Rishi (Sage) Beas and Kund is hindi word for tiny lake. This tiny lake of Beas Kund situated at higher and upper valley of Solang. Which is the heart of winter and summer games in Manali. It’s an ideal base camp for climbing nearby peaks Shitidhar, friendship and Ladakhi peak. The West side of Beas Kund is magnificent peak of Hanman Tibba 5990m, which access through tiny pass of above this lake. Beas Kund is a small holy lake with a small meadow beside it. The legend is that Rishi Vyash meditated here.

 

SKETCH ITINERARY:

 

DAY 01 :- Arrival Chandigarh & Transfer to Manali

Arrival Chandigarh Rail/Bus Station. Be greeted by tour guide who shall accompany you throughout this tour. Board coach to drive 250 km/8 hrs to our Camp at Manali.

Arrive  at  Camp Manali. Warm welcome with hot tea. Accommodation allotment. Issue of equipment and introduction of Camp Rules. Rest of the day at leisure with permissible activities. Tea and refreshment. Games, Campfire in evening. Dinner and Overnight stay at the camp.                                                                                                     

 

DAY 02 :- At Camp Manali

Warm up. After breakfast activities- FOREST FAMILIARIZATION, TWIG COLLECTION, INITIALIZATION TREKKING, EQUIPMENT DETAILING, KNOT TYING PRACTICE and TENT PITCHING. Get involved in the Rope Course activity- VALLEY CROSSING, BURMA BRIDGE with another TREK to tone your muscle for the tough trek ahead. Return to the camp for lunch. Team Building Activities like TWIG COLLECTION and BLIND FOLD etc. Tea and refreshment. Games, Campfire, Dinner. Overnight stay at the camp.                                                                                                     

 

Day 3: Manali- Solan Nallah (2840 mt./ 8 km drive)- Dhundi (Trek 8 km)

08.00 am: Pack your trekking gear after breakfast. Board the waiting vehicle to reach the Solang Nallah. Start first day's trekking upon reaching at Solang. The trek follows Beas River many times crossing the stream from one to another side. The trek is a mix of moderate climbs and easy walks. Wading through the greenery one reaches to the base camp at Dhundi. We will stop to prepare the light lunch and a brief rest enroute. Set up your camp upon arrival. Rest of the day for Cultural Evening with Games, Dance, Music and Camp Fire. Overnight in tents.

 

Day 4: Dhundi - Beas Kund (3540 m/ Trek 6 km)

The today's trek is steep. One has to trek over loosely held boulders and moraines of a dying glacier. We set-up camp at Beas Kund. Rest of the day for Cultural Evening with Games, Dance, Music and Camp Fire. Overnight in tents.

 

NB: We may need to set up camp at Bakerthach instead of Beas kund subject to climatic and logistical conditions at the time of trek. The Beas Kund shall be trekked to and returned from on the following day.

 

Optional : Trek up to Tentu Pass (4995 mt.). This is a arduous stretch and heavily glaciated. From the pass you can see Kulu valley and Hanuman Tibba (5932 mt.) at close distance. Return to Beas Kund for overnight stay.

 

Day 5: Beas Kund - Solan - Manali

Pack your bags and windup your camp before breakfast. Trek down 14 km to reach Solang Valley. A hot lunch shall be prepared here upon reaching the valley. Enjoy (on your own) the available Adventure and fun filled sports like Para Gliding, Zorbing, ATV Ride, Horse Riding etc. at Solang. Board the waiting vehicle in evening to reach your dear camp at Manali. Tea and refreshment. Games, Campfire in evening. Dinner and Overnight stay at the camp.                                                                                                      

 

Day 6: Manali - Chandigarh

08.00 am: Farewells and Parting Messages. Checkout post breakfast with a resolve to return to your dear camp at the earliest. Drive back to Chandigarh. Lunch enroute. Tour ends upon reaching Chandigarh.
