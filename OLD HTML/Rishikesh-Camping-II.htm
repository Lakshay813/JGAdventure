ABOUT RISHIKESH

This whitewater river rafting and meditation capital of India is situated amid the dense forest cover of Saal at an altitude of 430 mt in Garhwal part of Uttarakhand. A highly motorable NH 54 (Delhi – Badrinath- Mana road) connects it to Delhi. The mighty but safe waters of the Ganga are ideal for river rafting for almost all age groups. The combination of step and pool makes it the ideal river for rafting in India. With many expert river runners with excellent safety standards, equipment and all logistic support, rafting has become a very popular, exciting yet safe adventure sport option.  Don't miss out on the ride of a lifetime.
