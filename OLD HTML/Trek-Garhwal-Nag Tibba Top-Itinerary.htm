TREKKING EXPEDITION TO NAGTIBBA TOP

 

 

Location:            Distt. Dehradun (Uttarakhand)

Base:                  Pantwari

Grade:                Soft

Altitude:             3040 m

Best Time:         Round the year

Suitable For:      14 Years and above

 

 

Nagtibba 3040m, Uttaranchal as the name suggests is the abode of the Nag Devta (Snake God). The shepherds of the Aglar Valley on the north side of Mussoorie hills, known as Jaunpur, come to this ridge top to worship the Nag Devta. Nagtibba offers an excellent view of the mountain peaks in all directions. To reach the top you have to pass through dense forests. There is enough space for camping in the meadow, and the water source is nearby. Nagtibba can be climbed from two sides. Most trekkers prefer the Pantwari side, which has a beautiful village symbolizing the culture of Jaunpur. From here begins the climb to the Nagtibba top. You can get down on the other side to the village of Devalsari 2772 m. From here the road at Thatyur is not far away.

 

We have included both the sides of climb in our itinerary-2.

 

SKETCH ITINERARY-1

 

Day 1:  Arrival Dehradun – Drive to Mussoorie (32 km/1.5 hrs)

            Arrival Dehradun Rail/Bus Station. Be greeted by tour guide who shall accompany you throughout this tour. Board the hill coach to drive 32 km/1.5 hrs to Mussoorie.

Arrival  Mussoorie. Check in hotel/camp. Warm welcome with hot tea. Accommodation allotment. Rest of the day at leisure to enjoy the vicinity and sightseeing. Dinner and Overnight stay at the hotel/camp.                                                                                                     

 

Day 2: Mussoorie – Pantwari (Drive 53 km/3 hrs) – Nagtibba Base [2500m, Trek 5 km] 

Checkout post breakfast. Drive to Pantwari [Via Kempty-Yamuna Bridge-Purola]. Trek 5 km to setup your camp-1 at Nagtibba base after reaching Pantwari. Rest of the day free to enjoy the vicinity with Games, Forest Familiarization and Camp Fire. Dinner and Overnight in tents.

 

Day 3: Trek to Nagtibba Summit  [3050m/10,007 ft; 3 km] – Base Camp

Warm up. After Breakfast trek to NAGTIBBA TOP. Trace your footsteps back to campsite before lunch. Rest of the day free to enjoy the vicinity with Games and Camp Fire. Dinner and Overnight in tents.

 

Day 4:          Camp – Pantwari [trek 5 km] – Dehradun [Drive 85 km] Warm up. Breakfast. Pack your camp gear to return to Pantwari village. Board coach to return to Dehradun. Sightseeing stop at KEMPTY FALL during your return.

           Arrival Dehradun in evening.

           The tour ends upon reaching Dehradun.
