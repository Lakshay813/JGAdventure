Sarain – Chur chandni Peak Trek : VIA SHIMLA - CHOPAL & SARAIN

 

Location:            Distt. Shimla (Himachal)

Base:                  Sarain/Rajgarh

Grade:                Moderate

Altitude:             3647 m

Best Time:         May to November

Suitable For:      15 Years and above

 

VEGETATION:   Forest types include Westem mixed Coniferous , Kharsu Oak and Alpine pastures. Forests of Deodar, Oak and other species

 

WILD LIFE SANCTUARY: Common Langur ,Rhesus Macaque ,Leopard, Himalayan Black Bear, Wild Boar Indian Muntjac, Musk Deer, Goral , Royle ‘s Pika , Indian Porcupine, Koklas Pheasant , Himalayan Monal,Variegated Laughing thrush, Chestnut –tailed Minal, Grey-crested Tit,Black –crested Tit, White-tailed Nuthatch, Himalayan Green Finch, Red-headed Bullfinch, Bull finch.

 

ABOUT PLACE: Situated in district Shimla of Himachal Pradesh is Choor Dhar, once known as Choor-Chandni, which holds attraction for its picturesque locales as well as its association with mythology. Legend has it that Bhima’s grandson Barbareek witnessed the Mahabharata war from this summit which rests at 12,000 feet above the sea level. The forest with its scenic beauty, the chirping of the birds and cool, fresh air enlivens one with energy and enthusiasm throughout the trek. The Shiv temple (also known as Shirgul Mahadev) at Churdhar is accessible only from May to November each year. Heavy snowfall during the remaining part of the year forces the closure of this temple. In Churr Dhaar temple there is not yet any idol established nor worship is done. It may be said that once Maharaja Shirgul visited Chur dhar and made a foundation of this temple. According to folk stories it is described and considered that ascetic Adi Shankaracharya on his pilgrimage through Badrinath and Kedarnath came to Dharmshala by only a single route that was chur dhar and he might have establish linga here. Unfortunately there is no written proof of its existence. Although this fact can be explained with full faith that it is established in between two water streams known as Ganga and yamuna. It is a place where pilgrims bathe and wash away their sins, purify their souls and give gratitude to lord shiva by pouring water on linga.

 

SKETCH ITINERARY:

 

DAY 01 :- Arrival Chandigarh & Transfer to Shimla

Arrival Chandigarh Rail/Bus Station. Be greeted by tour guide who shall accompany you throughout this tour. Board coach to drive 95 km/3 hrs to our Camp Resort at Shimla.

Arrive  at  Little Heart Camp Resort. Warm welcome with hot tea. Accommodation allotment. Issue of equipment and introduction of Camp Rules. Lunch. Discussion on Camp Philosophy. Post lunch activities- FOREST FAMILIARIZATION, TWIG COLLECTION, INITIALIZATION TREKKING, EQUIPMENT DETAILING, KNOT TYING PRACTICE and TENT PITCHING. Get involved in the Rope Course activity- VALLEY CROSSING, BURMA BRIDGE. Tea and refreshment. Games, Campfire and DJ in evening. Dinner and Overnight stay at the resort.                                                                                                     

 

DAY 02 :- At Camp  

Warm up. After breakfast go for a ROCK CLIMBING/RAPPELLING session on natural rocks with another trek to tone your muscle for the tough trek ahead.. Return to the resort for lunch. Team Building Activities like TREASURE HUNT, TRUST FALL, TWIG COLLECTION and BLIND FOLD etc. Tea and refreshment. Games, Campfire and DJ after evening tea. NIGHT TRAIL post dinner. Overnight stay at the resort.                                                                                                     

 

DAY 03 :- Camp– Sarain– Camp Base (Drive 57 km/3 hrs, Trek 10 km)

05.00 am: we board coach with packed breakfast to reach the picturesque town of Sarain. Finish your breakfast upon reaching at Sarain to early start for the base camp for the summit of Churdhar.

09.00 am: We start the uphill trek to the base camp location. Small villages, terraced fields and thin streams dot the route. The trek is a mix of  steep climbs and easy walks. Wading through the greenery in the terraced fields, one reaches to the base camp. The forest with its scenic beauty, the chirping of the birds and cool, fresh air enlivens one with energy and enthusiasm throughout the trek. We will stop to prepare the light lunch and a brief rest enroute. Set up your camp upon arrival.

Rest of the day for Cultural Evening with Games, Dance, Music and Camp Fire. Overnight in tents.

 

DAY 04 :- Base Camp - Chur Chandni peak 4 km – Base Camp 4 km

Post early breakfast we start uphill on the greater rocky and snow clad terrain to reach Chur Chandni Peak. Light Lunch upon reaching at the small village of Chur Dhar. Post lunch and brief rest we move for the last 1 km steep hike to the temple at the peak. Post Darshan we trace our foot steps back to base camp.

Rest of the day for Cultural Evening with Games, Dance, Music and Camp Fire. Overnight in tents.

 

DAY 05 :- Base Camp – Shimla Camp (Trek 10 km, Drive 57 km/3 hrs)

Pack your bags and windup your camp before breakfast. Trek down 10 km to reach Sarain. Prepare your lunch upon reaching at Sarain. Board your waiting coach post lunch to drive back to your Shimla camp.

Tea and refreshment upon arrival. Games, Campfire and DJ in evening. Dinner and Overnight stay at the resort.                                                                                                     

 

DAY 06 :- DEPARTURE

08.00 am: Farewells and Parting Messages. Checkout post breakfast with a resolve to return to your dear camp at the earliest. TOUR CONCLUDES
