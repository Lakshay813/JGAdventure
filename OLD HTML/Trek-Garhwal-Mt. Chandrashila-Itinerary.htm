TREKKING EXPEDITION Mt. CHANDRASHILA PEAK/CHOPTA

 

-THE MINI SWITZERLAND OF ASIA-

 

FOR CLASS VI ONWARDS

 

Duration: 5/6 Days Max Altitude: 4150 M Best Time: April to November Grade: Moderate Trek

 

TRIP THEME: Chopta (8250 ft) is the international role model for environmental conservation and a belt of 40 km is preserved with strict discipline to maintain the status of this greenest spread. Regarded as "Mini Switzerland" in Asian tourist circles, Chopta is one of the most mesmerizing alpine meadows in Garhwal Himalayas with excellent camping opportunities.

Moreover Uttarakhand sources almost all major rivers and forests of North India. To protect these rivers and forests from vanishing and save ourselves from extinction, we have to understand and preserve nature in it's purest form and encourage others for the cause.

This trip is designed to promote this understanding in the best possible and affordable ways.

ABOUT PLACES COVERED :

Rishikesh (1419 ft) is situated on the banks of river Ganga and surrounded on three sides by Himalayan-ranges. It is a great pilgrimage centre of India being the gateway to "CHAR DHAM" - the pilgrimage to Badrinath, Kedarnath, Gangotri and Yamunotri.

Tungnath temple (12144 ft) is situated atop the Chandranath Parvat on a 3 km steep hike from Chopta. In this 1000 years old temple of Shiva where the dome spans sixteen doors, a 2.5 feet tall idol of Adi Guru Shankaracharya is located along side the lingam. The peak of Tungnath is the source of three springs that from the river Akashkamini. The Nandadevi temple is also situated at Tungnath not far from the awe-inspiring Akash ganga water fall, so called, because the water looks as though it is descending from heaven. The highest Hindu shrine in the Himalayas, 3kms uphill from Chopta, Tungath is reached through a path that wends through alpine meadows and rhododendron thickets.

Chandrashila Summit (13645 ft) is a rock face 1 km above the temple of Tungnath (One of Panch Kedars and highest of all noted Hindu temples). The oak forest through which you trek to the summit abounds in a shy wild life especially Monal, foxes, peasants and musk-deer (The Kanchula Korak Musk Deer Sanctuary is 3 km only). The spectacular view of Nandadevi, Shivling, Bhagirathi, Chaukhamba Kedarnath and the Gangotri - Yamunotri peaks add to the splendor of this place.

Deoria Tal (8200 ft) is a sparkling lake surrounded by dense forest. The famous Himalayan trout are found In abundance In the lake fed by natural springs in it’s depths. The lake is about three kilometers in circumference. Also find a lovely view of the 23,OOO ft high Chaukhamba peaks.

Kedarnath (ITINERARY CODE :  UK/TRK-3 only) - Amidst the dramatic mountain-scape of the majestic Kedarnath range stands one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Kedar or Lord Shiva. Lying at an altitude of 3584 m on the head of river Mandakini, the shrine of Kedarnath is amongst the holiest pilgrimages for the Hindus. There are more than 200 shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva in the district itself, the most important one is Kedarnath.

 

SKETCH ITINERARY :

 

 

 

DAY 1   :  Arrival Haridwar

Arrival at Haridwar Rail/Bus Station. Be greeted by tour guide who shall accompany you throughout this tour. Coach transfer to hotel/camp. Rest of the day free to enjoy the vicinity and sightseeing. Recharge your batteries by making a grand camp fire and cultural show in evening. Overnight at hotel/camp.

 

DAY 2      : TO CHOPTA (HILL DRIVE 240 KM/Min. 8 Hrs)

08.00 am       : Checkout post early breakfast and Drive to Chopta.

AFTERNOON: Arrival camp. A hot buffet lunch awaits your arrival. Accommodation allotment and camp briefing after lunch. Evening free to enjoy the vicinity with Games, Forest Familiarization and Camp Fire. Dinner and Overnight in tents.

 

DAY 3      : CAMP –DEORIA TAL (DRIVE 20 KM/TREK 3 KM- 1 way)

Activity          : Early morning drive to sari village post breakfast. Start trekking 3 km uphill to Deoriatal. Hot lunch shall be served here. Trace back your footsteps to Sari and drive back to camp. Evening free for games and cultural entertainment back at camp. Dinner and Overnight in tents.

 

DAY 4 : CAMP - TUNGNATH- CHANDRASHILA SUMMIT (DRIVE 5 KM/TREK 4.5 KM- 1 way)

ACTIVITY: Post breakfast drive with packed lunch to Chopta and further a moderate climb of 3 km to Tunganath. Further up 1.5 km for the most breathtaking view of the high ­Himalayas from Chandrashila Peak. Trek and drive down to hotel in evening. Overnight at camp.

 

DAY 5 : CHOPTA – HARIDWAR

05.00 am : Drive back to school with packed breakfast. Lunch enroute.

03.00 pm : Arrival Haridwar.

Tour ends upon reaching at Haridwar.
