TREKKING TO VALLEY OF FLOWERS & HEMKUND SAHIB

FOR CLASS IX ONWARDS

 

 

 

Best Time: May to November

Location: Garhwal (Uttaranchal)
Base: Govind ghat
Max. Altitude: 4329 m above Sea Level

 

Altitude(in meters):
Joshimath 1875  Govindghat 1828, Pulna 1920, Bhyundhar 2239, Ghangharia 3048, Valley of Flowers 3658, Hemkund 4329

 

 

SKETCH ITINERARY:

 

DAY 1: Arrival Haridwar

           Arrival at Haridwar Rail/Bus Station. Be greeted by tour guide who shall accompany you throughout this tour. Coach transfer to hotel/camp. Rest of the day free to enjoy the vicinity and sightseeing. Recharge your batteries by making a grand camp fire and cultural show in evening. Overnight at hotel.

 

DAY 2: Haridwar - Joshimath 1874 m (Drive 290 KM/10 hrs)

           Early morning checkout after breakfast. Drive to reach Joshimath which is the winter abode of Lord Badrinath. Lunch enroute. Evening arrive at Joshimath. Check in hotel. Enjoy the vicinity on your own. Overnight stay.

 

DAY 3: Joshimath-Govindghat-Ghangariya 3049 m (Drive 22 KM/Trek 13 km)

           Checkout post early breakfast. Drive by your hill coach to reach Govindghat. Start trekking to reach Ghangaria with packed lunch. Ghangaria is also known as GOVIND DHAM, It is the base camp for visiting Hemkund Sahib and Valley of Flowers. Dinner and Overnight stay at camp/guest house.

 

DAY 4: Ghangariya- Valley Of Flowers  (Trek 6 km one way)

           Nature walk to the Valley of Flowers with packed lunch. The whole valley is dotted with a varieties of exotic flowers and other flora. The valley starts after a short trek of 2 km and deepens up to 30 km approx.  Return to Ghangria for dinner and overnight stay.

 

DAY 5: Ghangaria- Hemkund Saheb 4,329 m(Tough Trek 6 km)- Ghangaria

              Early in the morning after early breakfast start trekking to reach "HEMKUND SAHIB". One of the most revered of all Sikh Shrines, The Hemkund Sahib- worlds Highest Gurudwara. Enjoy an auspicious ‘Lungar Chakhna’ here. In the afternoon trek down to reach Ghangariya for overnight stay. Evening is free for rest & relaxation.

 

DAY 6: Ghangaria- Govindghat (trek 13 km) - Haridwar (Drive 308 km/11 hrs.)

05.00 am: Pack your camp and start return trek to Govindghat.

08.00 am: Finish your breakfast upon arrival at Govindghat and board coach to drive back to Haridwar. 

08.00 pm: Arrival Haridwar. Check in hotel/camp. Dinner & Overnight stay at camp/hotel.

                  

DAY 7: – Haridwar- Destination

08.00 am: Checkout hotel post breakfast.  Drive back to Rail/Bus Junction. TOUR ENDS HERE.
