Rishikesh Camping Tour Package


 BEACH CAMP RISHIKES

 

The mighty waters of the Ganga, are ideal for white water rafting. With many expert river runners with excellent safety standards and imported equipment and all logistic support, rafting has become a very popular, exciting yet safe adventure sport option. The whitewater river rafting capital of India is the stretch of the Ganges from Kaudiyala to Rishikesh where the river has 12 rapids over 36 kms, most of which are easily negotiable even by non swimmers.

The riverside holi Ganges Beach camp is nestled silently amongst the greenest hills of Rishikesh at Shivpuri amidst the dense forest cover, on the Rishikesh-Badrinath highway, at an altitude of 430 mts, Don't miss out on the ride of a lifetime. Savor the thrill of raw adventure or the relaxation of a float trip.

 

REACHING RISHIKESH:
Road : Rishikesh is 232 km from Delhi. It is well connected to Delhi by a highly motorable road via Meerut, Roorki, Haridwar and Rishikesh. Bus services to Rishikesh are available in plenty from Delhi on every 30 minutes from ISBT Kashmiri Gate, Delhi and near ISBT Anand Vihar, Delhi. Particularly there are regular services from Haridwar, which is just half-an- hour journey from Rishikesh. Rishikesh also offers bus services to almost all nearby major cities. A 24X7 taxi service is available both from Haridwar and Rishikesh. Dehradun is only 38 km from here and major to and fro transportation is available between both the cities.

Air : Nearest airport is Jyoligrant about 18 km from Rishikesh.

Rail : Haridwar is well connected by rail from Delhi and have an option between an overnight train and the daily express. Trains like Shatabdi, Jan Shatabdi, Mussoorie Exp. and several others ply up to Haridwar.
