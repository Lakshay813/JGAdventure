 ACCESS TO CAMP

Brentwood Sanctuary Camp & Resort is 42 km from Dehradun which is the nearest railhead and airport. It is 12 kms from Mussoorie. From the Library Chowk of Mussoorie follow the Kempty Fall road. After 10 kms turn left into the Lake mist gate. Follow the unpaved jungle road for 2 kms. We are situated at end of the road.

We arrange for transfers to and from the camp on request (charges to be confirmed).

 

THE ACCOMMODATION

Brentwood Sanctuary Camp & Resort provides accommodation in colorful deluxe tents and cottage rooms.

The tents are cozy and secure with printed interiors and attached bathroom with running water. The amenities inside the tents include electricity, twin beds with mattress and quilt, bedside table, wall to wall carpet and colorful mats. With stunning view of the fields and the valley, the verandah in front of the tents are just perfect for a warm cup of tea or a good read.

The rooms are wood paneled and elegantly furnished with attached modern bathrooms with geysers. Heating is provided in the tents and rooms in winters.
