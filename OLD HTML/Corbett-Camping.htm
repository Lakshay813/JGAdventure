CORBETT NATIONAL PARK  
 
OPERATIONAL PERIOD: Round the year.

GATED SAFARI: 15th November to 15th June

 

India's first and finest park spread over 520 sq. km. along the banks of the Ramganga river, just 300 kms. northeast of Delhi in the foothills of the Himalayas is the Corbett National Park.

 

With elevations in the Park ranging from 400m to 1210m there is a rich diversity in habitat. Animals of the Himalayas and those of peninsular India can both be seen here and over 50 mammal and 25 reptile species have been recorded at Corbett. Among the predators are the tiger, leopard, diverse species of lesser cats and the dhole - the wild dog.

Near Bijrani Gate, Aam Danda, III UKFDC Depot, Uttarakhand

Camp staff is trained in First Aid and equipped accordingly. However a qualified doctor  is on camp panel and his services can be obtained by paying his visiting fee. Good medical attendance with surgical care is available at Ramnagar.
