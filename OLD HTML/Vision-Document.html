METHODOLOGY

A mix of methodology such as briefings, group and individual activity, discussions, role-plays, games, instruments and simulation are pressed into service. Each program is divided in various stages of excursion days and activities thereby adjusting the requisite content of fun and education together while accommodating the contents of an itinerary. Ouhe life’s lessons and positive experiences at every step.    Call it- ‘Experiential Learning’. Every peak and every mountain’s stream holds a story to be explored. The unique experience of grounding our relationship to the natural world leaves behind the experiences with a sense of joy, hope and to satisfy the curiosity which, we must confess, increases with time.

ECOLOGICAL TRADITION THAT WE INCULCATE: We

  • Do not cut trees & destroy rocks.
  • Use LPG for cooking.
  • Do not hurt or trap animals.
  • Use dustbins for a proper waste cycling.
  • Avoid using any fire crackers or any other loud instruments. Enjoy the sound of river, birds, animals & wind rustling the leaves.
  • Respect the local people's sentiments, customs & traditions.
  • Maintain silence to avoid disturbing the animals and also to increase the sighting When on the jungle safari please.
  • Avoid using soap / detergents in the river.
  • Have proper drainage to prevent an uncontrolled spill. Our toilets & bathing are modern and eco friendly.
  • We lit small campfires to contain the damage to forest.

Hope you shall allow us to share our  experience of outdoors with you and your beloved ones.
