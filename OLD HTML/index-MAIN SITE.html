Welcome to JG Camps & Nature Resorts

It is an age of new adventures, new – found lands, travel and fun. The Indian Himalayan states mainly- J&K, Uttarakhand and Himachal symbolize the passion of this new era. The broadest spectrum of adventure sports coupled with unexplored virgin terrains that these two states offer is unparalleled elsewhere. With an unconquerable Himalaya all along and many of the lifeline rivers of India originating from these states, they have naturally become a hub for adventure tourism. You can invite yourselves to tame the highest mountain peaks here or simply entertain yourselves with a barrage of adventure sports available.

Especially Uttarakhand and Himachal, with their gigantic potential for almost all sorts of adventure explorations, have recently emerged as nucleus for world adventure and mountaineering activities. Given the vast land full of annihilating wildlife/greenest forests/steep mountains/glaciers/perennial rivers hosting multifaceted activities like Yoga & meditation, mountaineering, nature/adventure trekking, aero sports and water sports to international standard fixed camps, Uttarakhand and Himachal remain unexplored ever.....
