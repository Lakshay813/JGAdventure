TREKKING TO DAYARA BUGYAL-GIDARA

 

FOR CLASS IX AND ABOVE

 

Duration: 6 Days Max Altitude: 3050 M Best Time: April to November Grade: Moderate Trek  

 

 

Bugyal in the local language means “High Altitude Meadow”. Situated at a huge height of over 3000 mts above sea level, the Dayara Bugyal or high altitude meadow as known in the local dialect is a dream come true for nature lovers. Splendid scenic beauty from a breathtakingly high angle, Dayara Bugyal has retained its beauty thanks to its unreachable heights! Untouched by human civilization, the Dayara Bugyal offers a picturesque view of the lovely Himalayas.

One can easily plan a trek to the Dayara and also witness the wonderful Sheshnag temple on the way. For the brave hearts able to bear extreme winters, the Dayara Bugyal is an excellent ski joint during the winters. Dayara Bugyal is one of the most beautiful places in Uttaranchal. Situated at an elevation ranging from 10,000-12,000 ft. This vast meadow is second to none in natural beauty in Uttaranchal. During winter it provides excellent & one of the best ski slopes in India spread over an area of 28 sqkms. The view of Himalayas from here is breathtaking. There is a small lake (Barnala Tal) in the area, and to camp by its side is a memorable event. The road to Dayara Bugyal branches off near Bhatwari a place on Uttarkashi-Gangaotri road about 28 km From Uttarkashi. Vehicles can go up to the village of Barsu from where one has to trek a distance of about 8 km to reach Dayara and other is via village Raithal, 10 km from Bhatwari from where one has to trek about 6 km to Dayara Bugyal.

                            

SKETCH ITINERARY :

 

DAY 1: Arrival Haridwar

            Arrival at Haridwar Rail/Bus Station. Be greeted by tour guide who shall accompany you throughout this tour. Coach transfer to hotel/camp. Rest of the day free to enjoy the vicinity and sightseeing. Recharge your batteries by making a grand camp fire and cultural show in evening. Overnight at hotel/camp.

 

DAY 2 : Haridwar – Raithal/Barsu (1218 m/252 km/8 hrs drive)  

08.00 am: Checkout post early breakfast and drive to Raithal/Barsu via Uttarkashi (1,158 m). Stop enroute for lunch. Participate in ‘TENT PITCHING’ demo upon arrival at Raithal/Barsu. Use your training in pitching your own tent to setup your camping den. Evening free to enjoy the vicinity with GAMES & FOREST FAMILIARIZATION. Overnight in tents.

 

DAY 3 : Barsu – Barnala Tal (6500 ft / Trek 3 km)

            After breakfast start from Raithal / Barsu to Barnala Tal. Hot lunch to provide sufficient energy for another camp setup at Barnala Tal. Free to enjoy the vicinity with GAMES. Session for SELF COOKING under training from kitchen staff. Overnight in tents.

                                                                                                                                                     

DAY 4 : Barnala Tal – Dayara Bugyal (10000 ft / Trek 8 km)  

            After an early breakfast trek to Dayara Bugyal with packed lunch. Enjoy the panorama of Dayara before setting up your camp at Dayara. Free to enjoy the vicinity with GAMES. Overnight in tents.

                                                                                                                                                      

Day 5 : Dayara - Gidara (13,000 ft / Trek 7 km one way) - Dayara
Morning after breakfast return trek to Gidara with packed lunch. Trek back to Dayara after exploring the untouched beauty of Gidara. Dinner & Overnight at Dayara Camp.

 

Day 6 : Dayara – Raithal/Barsu - Haridwar

05.00 am: Pack your bags and trek down to Raithal/Barsu.

08.00 am: Drive back to Haridwar from Raithal/Barsu post breakfast. Lunch enroute.

05.00 pm  : Arrival Haridwar.       

Tour ends upon reaching at Haridwar.
